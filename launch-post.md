## Primary (full story)

For years, this is how I tracked my workouts. 📅

A printed calendar, a pen, and little tally marks for every set. No app, no subscription — just me trying to stay in the groove. (You can see the months I fell off, too. That's kind of the point.)

I kept going back to that paper calendar because it was simple and it never made me feel bad about the days I missed. I just always wished I had it on my phone.

So I built it. It's called **TackT** — a free, phone-first workout tracker for getting back into training after falling off. Log a workout in a few taps, watch the calendar fill in. No ads, no guilt, no pressure. Just today.

It's the first app I've ever put out publicly, and honestly I built it for me — but if you've been meaning to get back in the groove, I'd love for you to try it and tell me what you think.

👉 **gettackt.com** — free, opens right in your browser, add it to your home screen.

One day at a time.
